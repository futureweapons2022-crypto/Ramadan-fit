THANK YOU FOR PURCHASING RAMADAN MODE.

This is a standalone, privacy-focused application. It does not require an app store, a subscription, or an internet connection to function once loaded.


HOW TO INSTALL (iOS & ANDROID)
- This app is designed to work as a "Progressive Web App" (PWA).This means it runs like a native app without the App Store.

- Send the "RamadanMode_Gumroad_Edition.html" file to your phone.(You can email it to yourself, use AirDrop, or save it to iCloud/Google Drive).
- Open the file on your phone.iOS: Open in the Files app, then tap the "Share" icon and select "Open in Safari".Android: Open in Chrome.
- Add to Home Screen (CRITICAL STEP).Once open in the browser, tap the "Share" button (iOS) or "Menu" dots (Android).Select "Add to Home Screen".Name it "Ramadan Fit" and tap "Add".
- Open the new Gold Icon on your home screen.The app will now launch in full-screen "Pro Mode" with the premium intro animation.

FEATURES & TIPSPRIVACY FIRST: 

- All your data (weight, workouts, nutrition) is saved directly to your device's memory. We cannot see your data.
- OFFLINE MODE: Prayer times are calculated mathematically on your device. You do not need internet for the countdowns to work.
- BATTERY SAVER: The dark "OLED" theme is designed to minimize battery usage during long days.
- If you reset your phone's browser cache ("Clear History and Website Data"), your workout logs may be cleared. We recommend using the "Export Data" button in the Settings menu weekly to backup your progress.
